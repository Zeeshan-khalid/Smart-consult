Evaluation

  • We provide evaluations of cost, pricing and reimbursement systems for the health economy

  • Information on the determination of prices of hospital products and identification of possible reimbursement issues or barriers in the UK which could impact the obtaining of a premium price for health services and products

  • Evaluation of the impact of Patient Decision Aids on changes in activity, including numbers of procedures. This provides a view of the impact on procedures, but not on any impact on less invasive management options.

  • Measuring Shared Decision Making in local health economies to provide a summary of the types of measures that are available, a recommendation of which measures might be appropriate for the programme and a rationale for the suggested approach from a dyadic perspective i.e. from the point of view of both the service user and clinician.
