Primary Care Services

We provide support to primary care providers, commissioners and system partners to deliver high quality clinically-led primary care and out of hospital services by working with local clinicians, service users, and other partner organisations to ensure the sustainability and development of a vibrant primary care service offer to meet the needs of the local population.

Some of the services we provide include:

- Reviewing practice finances and assistance with practice financial planning e.g. to identify any discrepancies or anomalies in income or costs and to make appropriate recommendations to reduce costs and increase income

- Writing, reviewing and submitting of procurement bids

- Preparation / review of business cases for commissioners e.g. for mergers / closures of practices or branches, or to access new funding sources

- GMS / PMS and APMS Contract advice

- Help with negotiations, including for GP practice mergers, splits and closures

- Undertaking patient demand and practice capacity audits, including in-depth review and analysis of the results, with recommendations to reduce patient demand and increase GP capacity

- Analysis of reception team activity, including in-depth review of the results, with recommendations to reduce demand and increase capacity in reception

- Providing support to practices on all premises-related matters, including relocation, new-build, refurbishment and funding access and allocation
