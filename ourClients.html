OUR CLIENTS

We work with fantastic organisations across the public sector where our expertise solves problems for public services.

National Consultation on NHS Programme of Improvement to Radiotherapy services in England.

This involved a national consultation seeking views on the creation of 11 radiotherapy networks in England which would increase access to dedicated subspecialist teams to ensure access to more innovative radiotherapy treatments and ensure radiotherapy equipment is fully utilised. The final report provides a thematic review of the survey with regional, national, service users and clinician themes extracted and cross-referenced against each other; qualitative and quantitative analysis of the survey data using various techniques including the use of expert software ; and publication of a national report in a meaningful, balanced and accessible manner, making appropriate use of visual aids such as maps, scales and graphs, while providing notable quotes and references.

Whole Systems in Care (My Care My Way) – Financial and Contract Management including Financial gap analysis of the financial challenge being encountered within the West London area; Forecast of Long Term Financial trend models; and the calculation of Return on Investment (ROI) for the business case.

The collection, review and presentation of Health Education England’s Education and Training Cost Collection Exercise across all Imperial College Health Trust sites, involving the survey of all medical, dental and nursing staff, analysis of the survey results and a costing exercise to determine the annual cost of training to the Trust.

Planning all Care Together (PaCT) Project – 28 Out of Hospital initiatives which radically redesign community health and adult social care services while transforming primary care services. Provision of Financial and Contract Management including Financial gap analysis; Forecast of Long Term Financial trend models; and the calculation of Return on Investment (ROI) for the business case.

Review of assumptions in relation to bed occupancy in line with Royal College standards and the assessment of capital impacts to understand the financial implications of proposed new builds.

Financial assessment of income projections based on both demographic/non-demographic growth including the implications of inflation/deflation assumptions.
