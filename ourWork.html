What We Do

We identify, implement and embed effective and sustained change by providing expert advice, consultancy, training, and project and programme management. Working alongside you, we help you to set the right strategies, develop pragmatic plans, deliver change, develop capability and address operational issues.

Analysis

The first step in our process is to undertake a credible analysis of client-specific data. This process includes but is not limited to identifying any quick wins where increases in productivity and efficiency could be made immediately. These are always validated by experts on the ground. This is followed by an optimised review to align performance to potential capacity and look for improvement opportunities.

Rapid Change

Our implementation programme is based around results from week one. First on the cards is the realisation of quick wins agreed with during the analysis. The speed of these programmes is designed to maximize the Full Year Effect while establishing quick wins as an essential step in providing direction and focus.

Implementation

The implementation phase sees our experienced clinical and operational delivery leads working hands-on with staff to share knowledge and expertise. They will also target and tackle any operational issues and blockers preventing required productivity improvements. Our approach will help provide pace and strengthen operational grip, in a sustainable way. Practical tools and techniques are deployed to immediately begin changing practices.

Monitoring

Following Implementation we can continue to offer arms-length support through remote monitoring of performance, while offering regular reports using our ‘in-session’ productivity approach to highlight performance and progress against the established efficiency improvement targets. Using Smart Consult’s proven processes and applications mean clients can help translate the significant efficiencies we find into a sustainable reality as well as creating a high ROI.

Sustainability

Is at the core of our business ethos. Whether it’s through our custom-designed capacity management solutions or a light touch continuous service we strive to provide continued support of operational improvements and future service redesign.
